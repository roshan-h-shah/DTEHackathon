About Slightly

At Slightly, our mission is to create innovative, accessible solutions that support individuals on their mental health journey, empowering them to lead fulfilling, balanced lives. By integrating technology with compassionate care, we aim to break the stigma around mental health and ensure that everyone has access to the tools and resources they need to thrive.

Our focus is on fostering a supportive community that champions emotional resilience, self-awareness, and holistic healing, making mental health a top priority in everything we do.

Our Innovative Approach

Our product leverages a powerful model built on an extensive Quantitative EEG dataset from more than 850 patients to accurately assess mental health disorders. By analyzing raw EEG data and then transforming this into Quantitative EEG data, our system can predict whether a person is likely to be suffering from Addictive, Anxiety, Mood, Obsessive Compulsive, Schizophrenia and Stress-related disorders.

This data-driven approach enables individuals to make informed decisions quickly, and with minimal cost. Our goal is to revolutionize mental health diagnostics through the power of AI and advanced EEG analysis.
